About Me.

My journey to Computer Science started in high school when I had a mandatory programming class. In this class, I didn’t write with an actual programming language yet; instead, I played with blocks. All I had to do was build upon those blocks by attaching them to each other to create a program. It was like legos to me-- fun and creative. I had the freedom to make whatever I wanted, and that’s what I love about programming.

I then took more classes related to programming. My passion for coding really took off when I was given yet another chance of freedom to make whatever I wanted for my final project. I built my own game, and I was really proud of myself in the end. Throughout that process, I was learning more than I needed, improving my project beyond what was taught in the curriculum, and was genuinely satisfied seeing the result. This very experience helped me decide to pursue Computer Science in college.

Now, I continue to explore new areas of coding, whether it's learning frontend, backend, cybersecurity or anything else that piques my interest. I will continue my journey to grow and improve my skills even further.
